Overview of the Brain and Its Importance
The brain is one of the most important and complex part of the human body. It's a three-pound organ that is at the seat of intelligence, interpreter of our senses, the initiator of our body movement and controller of all human behavior. It is this organ that is the source of all the qualities that define our humanity. This app aims to give you a general overview about the brain and hopefully will give you a better understanding of its importance. Anyone can learn about their brain just use it and you will be learning in no time. Enjoy your journey into the brain.
